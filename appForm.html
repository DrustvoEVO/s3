Application form

Fill in the form below to apply! You will need to include your personal information, as well as your motivational letter and, in case you are applying for our scholarship, a personal statement explaining your family's financial situation. Also prepare the contacts of a parent/guardian and a teacher (not necessarily in a STEM subject).

As you will likely not be able to save your progress, do not start filling out the application before you have your letter(s) ready to paste into the appropriate text box.
