Covid-19 measures

General information

In regards to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, we would like to assure you that the safety of our participants and staff is of the utmost importance to us. We will ensure that the interaction at the School complies with the recommendations of both the local and European health authorities. We will do our best to prevent an outbreak at the School, and in case it happens, we will provide separate accommodation for the participants that need to be isolated.

Prevention

Each staff member of the School will be fully vaccinated and tested on the first and the fifth day of the School. While we do not require our participants to be vaccinated, we will absolutely require that they be tested with an antigen self-test on the first and the fifth day. The price of the self-tests is included in the fee and therefore the testing will not incur any additional costs to the participants.

Official tests

The School will not cover or arrange official antigen or PCR tests required for the travel arrangements of the participants, although we will be happy to assist you with any information on this matter. In case that the School is cancelled due to the COVID-19 measures, we will fully return the participation fee to each participant. However, we will not be able to refund the participants for their travel arrangements.
